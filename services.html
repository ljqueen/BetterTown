Our Programs

Student Program

We help students get recognized by colleges and provide opportunities for academic growth and achievement. Our program connects students with scholarships, awards, and recognition events.

Rebuilding Program

We work to improve and update the look of our town through community-driven projects, including fixing public spaces and organizing trash cleanups. Join us in making our town a better place to live!

Community Program

We host and plan community events in partnership with the government of Oceana, WV. Our goal is to bring people together, foster connections, and create lasting positive change through local events and initiatives.
